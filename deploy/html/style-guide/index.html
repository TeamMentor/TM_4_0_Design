Visual Identity

Our visual identity is an essential part of the TEAM Mentor Brand Licence and is cast in stone. Stick to the guidelines to present a uniform image to the world and create a strong brand.


Logo

Our logo is at the heart of all communication aimed at our users.




Written Style

In body text, TEAM Mentor should always appear in Helvetica (no bold, italics, outline or underline) with the word "team" in uppercase followed (with a space) by the word "mentor", only the initial letter of which is capitalised.


Minimum Size

The logo should always be clearly legible.

How to use our colours



White should only be used a background colour or font colour in places where there are colours or images as backgrounds (eg. Landing Page).


The brand colour should be used for the company name and for action links (save, login, start your free trial etc.) as well as icons.



The dark green should be used for anything that is active or on hover, but only when it starts in the TM green. This colour is very heavy, so it is never used as a headline or link colour.



The light grey should be used as the lead colour for the opening headline on a page. An article title, filters headline, navigate options, user settings etc. The first thing that users read will be in this colour.



The dark grey is the main colour in the application. Body copy, article sub-headlines, related article headlines, options (filters), inputs and forms etc all use this colour.



Black is used very sparingly as it’s harsh on a straight white background. Only use it for real emphasis and always try to use the dark grey first.
